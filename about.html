Coffin will notify you of your need to rest and that even vampires need healthy sleep.

Coffin is inspired by http://sleepyti.me which calculates optimal times to sleep based on your desired time to wake.

This application notifies you throughout the night of the optimal times to close the laptop and get to sleep, providing a button to do so.
